Modern Coach Factory, Raebareli

Passenger Coach Manufacturing Website

Design — Frontend

Indian Railways (reporting mark IR) is an Indian state-owned enterprise, Owned and operated by the government of India through the Ministry of Railways. It is one of the world’s largest railway networks. CRIS (Centre for Railway Information Systems) is an organization under the Ministry of Railways. CRIS is a unique combination of competent IT professionals and experienced Railway personnel enabling it to deliver complex Railway IT systems. The Modern Coach Factory (MCF) in Raebareli, India is a railway passenger coach manufacturing unit. It was setup by the Indian Railways in 2007 and was named as Modern Coach Factory in 2015.

Earlier, Employees in Modern Coach Factory, Raebareli used to maintain all their data manually on excel sheets, thereby making it a tedious and inefficient task. Therefore, the idea of making software for handling the entire MCF project was proposed to minimize the paperwork and make it an efficient as well as easy to handle application. Modern Coach Factory, Raebareli is one of application software that provides facility to employees through the web based application. The Centre for Railway Informations System is developing a web based application for the Modern Coach Factory situated in Raebareli. The system is proposed to facilitate the employees to maintain the records of the manufacturing done at various shops, shifts of the employees, jobs allocated to the employees and to have a glimpse of the overall performance of the system with the help of various reports provided.

 

Student Associations and Clubs
A Banasthali Portal

College Website

Design — Frontend

Student Associations and Clubs - A Banasthali Portal is an online club management system web application that serves the functionality of club management. It replaces the manual working of the clubs of Banasthali Vidyapith and provides a single online platform where all the clubs can display their operationalization. The system allows all the students of Banasthali Vidyapith to view the events and workshops conducted by different clubs, register for them at the same time, give their feedback and ask queries. All the data is logged into the database and only those students can register whose information can be fetched from the college database. Although the website is open to all, if a visitor’s details are not present in the college database, she won’t be able to register for any event or workshop. This data is sent to the administrator (Banasthali Vidyapith).

Student Associations and Clubs is a public domain open to all, where all the workings of all the clubs are organized in a professional, focused and creative way. It’s target audience is the students of Banasthali Vidyapith. For a very long time the functioning of the clubs has been done manually and in a scattered form. This online platform provides a way to unite all the clubs and makes it much easier for the students to connect with the clubs as well as club members.

 

Netflix' Recommendation System

Seminar Presentation

Theoretical Research

Netflix is defined at the point where the Internet and Narratives converge. It aims at developing Internet television. Their main product and source of revenue is a subscription service that allows members to stream any video in their collection of movies and TV shows at any time on a wide range of Internet-connected devices

Since the internet television space is young and the competition is top notch, Netflix developed a personalized hybrid recommender system that helps its members to find videos in every session. It delivers personalized recommendations to its users. Netflix makes use of one of the most advanced and complex recommendation system. Being a Subscription-based streaming service, its recommender system is not just one algorithm but a collection of different algorithms. The recommender system has a personalized homepage for each of its members where their goal is to help the members find the content they are interested in.

The report explains the Multi-Armed Bandit testing approach that Netflix uses for its recommendations and one of its famous strategies, the epsilon-greedy strategy. The report also gives an overview of the system framework of Netflix’s recommender system which includes two main processes – Offline computation and Online Computation.
